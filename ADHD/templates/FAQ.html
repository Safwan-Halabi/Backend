Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ page! Here, you'll find answers to common questions about our products, services, and policies. We're dedicated to providing clear and up-to-date information.


Is this app a replacement for a professional ADHD diagnosis?

No, this app is for informational purposes only. It cannot diagnose ADHD. If you suspect you may have ADHD, please consult with a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis.

What kind of data does the app collect?

The app saves the eye-tracking results and voice-analysis (Frequency and Pitch but not the voice recording itself ) and the Questionnaire results. No further results will be saved for privacy reasons.

How is my data protected?

All communications between the App, our Server and our Database are based on secure connections.

What should I do if I think I might have ADHD?

Contact a medical professional (Doctor or Neurologist) and request a medical ADHD diagnosis.

Is this app appropriate for children?

Yes, this app is tailor-made for ADHD detection in children. It includes comprehensive questionnaires for parents, ensuring they are an integral part of the ADHD detection process by providing valuable insights and information about their child's behavior and symptoms.
