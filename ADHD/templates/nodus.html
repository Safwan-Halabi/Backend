About Us

At ADHD Detection, we offer a virtual platform for detecting ADHD symptoms in children using modern available technology. Our platform is designed to be simplistic and easy-to-navigate, with a user-friendly focus. Our unique selling point is our innovative approach to detecting ADHD symptoms. We provide eye-tracking tests, vocal tests using a webcam and microphone, as well as reaction time tests using a keyboard. Our platform is open 7 days a week and accepts payments from all major insurance plans. No appointments needed!
